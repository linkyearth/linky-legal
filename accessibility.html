Accessibility Policy for Linky

Last Updated: December 15, 2024

At Linky, we are committed to ensuring that our app and services are accessible to everyone, including individuals with disabilities. Our goal is to provide an inclusive platform that meets a wide range of accessibility needs, guided by global standards and best practices.

1. Commitment to Accessibility

Linky is dedicated to making our services user-friendly and accessible to all. We continually work to improve the user experience for everyone by aligning with accessibility standards such as the WCAG 2.1, Level AA.

2. Accessibility Features

  • Screen reader compatibility for navigating and interacting with the app.
  • Adjustable font sizes for improved readability.
  • High-contrast options to enhance visual clarity.
  • Keyboard and voice navigation support, where applicable.

3. Feedback and Contact

We welcome your feedback to help us improve accessibility. If you encounter barriers or have suggestions for improvement, please contact us:

  • Email: accessibility@linky.earth
  • Phone: +1-800-ACCESS
  • Mail: Accessibility Team, Linky.earth LLC, [Address]

4. Ongoing Efforts

  • Regular audits and updates to improve accessibility features.
  • Incorporating user feedback to address accessibility challenges.
  • Training our team to prioritize inclusivity in design and development.

5. Compliance Standards

While we strive to conform to WCAG 2.1, Level AA, some parts of our app may not yet meet these standards. We are actively working to address these gaps and ensure a more accessible experience for all users.

6. Third-Party Tools and Content

Some content on Linky, including third-party tools or integrations, may not fully meet accessibility standards. We encourage users to provide feedback on such instances, and we will work with providers to improve accessibility.

7. Updates to This Policy

This policy may be updated periodically to reflect improvements or changes in accessibility efforts. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will indicate the latest revision.

Thank you for helping us create a more inclusive and accessible Linky experience. Your input and collaboration are invaluable as we strive to serve everyone better.
