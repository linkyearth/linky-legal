Community Guidelines for Linky

Last Updated: December 15, 2024

At Linky, we aim to build a vibrant and respectful community where Linkers can share, explore, and contribute content freely. To maintain this environment, we’ve established these Community Guidelines. By using Linky, you agree to follow these guidelines to help foster a positive and safe space for everyone.

1. Respect for Others

  • Treat all Linkers with respect and courtesy, regardless of their background, opinions, or contributions.
  • Do not engage in harassment, hate speech, bullying, or any form of discriminatory behavior, including but not limited to racism, sexism, or xenophobia.
  • Avoid targeting individuals with threats, insults, or offensive content.

2. Appropriate Content

Prohibited Content: Do not upload or share content that:

  • Is illegal, defamatory, or obscene.
  • Contains nudity, sexually explicit material, or graphic violence.
  • Promotes terrorism, hate groups, or harmful organizations.
  • Spreads misinformation, spam, or malicious software.

Respect Intellectual Property: Only share content that you own or have the legal right to distribute. Do not plagiarize, infringe on copyrights, or violate trademarks.

3. Tagging and Contributions

  • Use tags responsibly and accurately to describe content.
  • Avoid creating tags or comments that are offensive, misleading, or irrelevant to the content.
  • Ensure all comments, links, and images align with these guidelines and add value to the community.

4. Privacy and Safety

  • Do not post private or sensitive information about others without their consent.
  • Protect your own privacy by avoiding unnecessary personal disclosures in public posts.
  • Report suspicious or harmful behavior immediately to our moderation team.

5. Geolocation and Shared Spaces

  • Use location features responsibly and accurately.
  • Do not use geolocation to stalk, harass, or harm others.

6. Fair Use of Features

  • Do not misuse features for spam, manipulation, or fraud.
  • Avoid creating multiple accounts to skew votes or visibility.

7. Reporting and Enforcement

  • Use app tools or contact support@linky.earth to report violations.
  • Violations may result in removal, suspension, or banning of accounts.

8. Compliance with Laws

  • Always comply with local, national, and international laws.
  • You’re responsible for ensuring your content abides by legal standards.

9. Moderation and Updates

  • We may remove or restrict content or accounts that disrupt the community.
  • Guidelines may be updated; changes will be noted with the “Last Updated” date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about these Community Guidelines, please contact us:

  • Email: support@linky.earth
  • Address: Linky.earth LLC

Thank you for being a valued member of the Linky community. Let’s work together to create an inspiring, respectful, and enjoyable environment for all!
